Minecraft: 18 Tricks From The Game You May Not Know

These are some tips and tricks for Minecraft that even seasoned players may not be aware of. Whether you're a newcomer or a veteran, there's always something new to discover!

  • Increasing Llama Carrying Capacity: Use llamas to transport items by clicking on them with a chest. Breeding llamas can increase their inventory space.
  • Blocks Break On The Northmost Face: When you break a block, the breaking pattern on the top face points north. Useful for orientation.
  • Lava Buckets Are Great Fuel: Collect lava in buckets; it's an excellent fuel for furnaces and can also create obsidian.
  • New Diamond Levels: In Minecraft 1.18, diamonds spawn differently, so explore new Y-levels for better chances.

Remember, even if you know some of these tricks, there are others who are still learning. Happy mining!
